Arcade

Welcome to the ultimate destination for fun and excitement – our arcade! Step into a world where the thrill of classic games meets the latest in gaming technology. Whether you're a pinball wizard, a master of air hockey, or a retro video game enthusiast, there's something here for everyone. Our vibrant and lively atmosphere is perfect for families, friends, and gamers of all ages. Come play, laugh, and win – because at our arcade, the fun never ends!

Game Selection

Hoop Dreams

Test your shooting skills in this basketball game. With dynamic scoring, challenge your friends to see who can make the most baskets in a timed round.

Pac-Man: Retro Revival

Relive the nostalgia with Pac-Man: Retro Revival. Navigate the maze, gobble up pellets, and avoid ghosts in this updated version of the beloved classic. Can you achieve the high score?

Pinball Wizard

Experience the magic of Pinball Wizard, a state-of-the-art pinball machine with dazzling lights, sound effects, and intricate playfields. Rack up points and unlock special features as you master the game.

Fast and the Furious: Street Racer

Buckle up and hit the streets in this adrenaline-pumping racing game. Choose your car, customize it, and race against others in high-speed, action-packed tracks inspired by the Fast and the Furious franchise.

Pirate's Treasure Hook

mbark on a swashbuckling adventure with Pirate's Treasure Hook. Use the hook to snag treasure chests and collect gold coins. Aim carefully and time your moves to maximize your haul.

Milk Jug Toss Mania

Step right up and test your aim with Milk Jug Toss Mania. Throw balls into the milk jugs to score points and win tickets. It's a fun and challenging game that brings the carnival experience to the arcade.

Skee-Ball Showdown

Roll the balls up the ramp and aim for the high-scoring targets in Skee-Ball Showdown. Compete against friends or try to beat your own high score in this timeless arcade favorite.

Human Claw Machine

Step into the giant claw machine and become the claw! Use the controls to maneuver yourself and grab as many prizes as you can. It's a thrilling and interactive twist on the classic arcade game.

Galactic Defender: Space Invaders

Defend the galaxy from waves of alien invaders in this modern take on the classic Space Invaders game. With enhanced graphics and immersive sound effects, you'll feel like you're in the middle of an interstellar battle.

Nerf Blaster

Dive into the action with Nerf Blaster! Take aim at moving targets with your Nerf blaster in this interactive and immersive game. With various levels of difficulty and dynamic elements, it's perfect for Nerf enthusiasts of all ages. Compete for the highest score and enjoy endless fun.
