How we started

Billy and Mac, childhood friends with a shared passion for cooking, decided to turn their dream of owning a restaurant into reality in Warren, Ohio. After years of working in various kitchens and perfecting their culinary skills, they pooled their savings and found a cozy spot in their hometown. They transformed it into BillyMac Burgers, a unique eatery that combined their love for great food with fun entertainment. To make their restaurant stand out, they included an arcade featuring games like pinball, air hockey, and more. This innovative idea attracted families and friends looking for a place to enjoy delicious burgers and have fun. Their dedication to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and their commitment to quality quickly earned them a loyal customer base, making BillyMac Burgers a beloved local hangout.

About the owners

Encouraged by the success of their Warren location, Billy and Mac decided to expand BillyMac Burgers to Youngstown, Ohio. They believed that their unique combination of great food and arcade entertainment would be well-received in the neighboring city. The new location featured an even larger arcade with more games and a spacious dining area to accommodate the growing number of customers. Billy continued to create mouthwatering burger recipes, while Mac ensured the business ran smoothly and maintained its friendly atmosphere. The expansion to Youngstown proved to be a smart move, as the new BillyMac Burgers quickly became a popular spot for locals and visitors alike. Their innovative approach and dedication to quality helped them build a strong community presence in both cities.
