Welcome to BillyMac Burgers!

At BillyMac Burgers, our mission is to create a welcoming and vibrant community space where delicious food and fun come together. We are dedicated to serving high-quality, mouthwatering burgers made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the kitchen, as we strive to provide exceptional service and a memorable dining experience for every guest. With our unique blend of great food and arcade entertainment, we aim to bring joy and excitement to families and friends, fostering connections and creating lasting memories. At BillyMac Burgers, we believe in the power of good food and good times to bring people together.

Top 10 Fan Favorites

Swiss Mushroom Pretzel Burger

Delight in our 1/3lb burger, featuring a juicy beef patty topped with earthy cremini mushrooms, melted Swiss cheese, and caramelized grilled onions. Complemented by fresh lettuce and a rich bacon onion jam, all nestled in a toasted pretzel bun, this burger offers a savory and satisfying experience with every bite!

Decadent Black Forest Cake Slice

Treat yourself to a generous slice of our rich Black Forest cake, featuring layers of moist chocolate sponge, luscious real cream whipped cream, and tart cherries. Topped with chocolate shavings, chocolate bark, and a cherry garnish, this classic dessert is a perfect blend of sweetness and indulgence.

Guacamole Delight Burger

Savor the flavors of our 1/3lb burger, featuring a succulent beef patty topped with creamy guacamole and crisp red onion. Nestled in a toasted whole wheat bun, this burger offers a perfect blend of freshness and richness, making it a delightful choice for any guacamole enthusiast!

St. Louis-Style Spareribs

Hand-rubbed secret blend of spices and pit-smoked for 3 - 4 hours over a smoldering hickory fire. Then slathered with sauce over an open flame to seal in the flavor with a crispy, caramelized coating.

Flavorful Bone-In Wings

Enjoy our delicious wings, available in your choice of hot, mild, or honey drizzled. Each order is served with crisp celery and carrot sticks, and your choice of creamy Ranch or tangy Bleu Cheese dressing. Perfect for sharing or savoring solo, these wings are sure to satisfy your cravings!

The Ultimate Bacon Bliss Burger

Indulge in our mouthwatering 1/2lb double burger, stacked high with juicy beef patties, crispy applewood smoked bacon, and melted American cheese. Topped with savory bacon jam, fresh lettuce, crunchy pickles, ripe tomato, and sliced onion, all drizzled with creamy bacon aioli. Served on a perfectly toasted potato bun, this burger is a bacon lover's dream come true!

Vanilla Strawberry Chantilly Delight

Indulge in a large slice of our Vanilla Strawberry Chantilly cake, featuring layers of fluffy vanilla sponge cake, fresh strawberries, and light Chantilly cream. This delightful dessert is topped with a generous amount of cream and a strawberry garnish, offering a perfect balance of sweetness and freshness.

Ultimate Sundae Delight

Dive into our Ultimate Sundae Delight, featuring creamy ice cream topped with colorful sprinkles, a large wafer cookie, and a rolled cookie wafer. Finished with crunchy nuts and a cherry on top, this sundae is available in your choice of strawberry, chocolate, or caramel sauce. Perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth!

Tropical Coconut Cake Slice

Delight in a large slice of our Tropical Coconut cake, featuring layers of moist coconut-flavored sponge cake and creamy coconut frosting. Topped with shredded coconut for an extra touch of paradise, this cake offers a perfect blend of sweetness and tropical flavor.

Oreo Cookies & Cream Dip-N-Dots

Experience the fun and flavor of our Cookies & Cream Dip-N-Dots ice cream. These tiny, creamy beads of ice cream are packed with the classic taste of Oreo cookies and cream, offering a delightful twist on a beloved favorite. Perfect for a cool treat any time!
